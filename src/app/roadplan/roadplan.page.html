You Can Get Almost Anywhere in Goa Price of ticket

Price of the ticket ranging from 10 INR (about 0,15 EUR/USD) for a shorter ride to 40 INR for a half across the state journey on an express bus.

Motorcycle Taxi

It consists of a man riding a two wheeler and takes you as the pillion rider. This mode of transport is quite swift and takes you even through heavy traffic. It is locally known as "Pilot".
